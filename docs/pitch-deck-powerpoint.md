# Companion

A wellness app that notices when your daily patterns change and helps before stress builds up.

- No mood journal required
- Works quietly
- Privacy first
- Built to demo

# The Problem

Wellness apps usually depend on the hardest habit: remembering to check in.

- People forget to log how they feel
- Stress is often noticed too late
- A phone app alone sees only part of the routine

# The Whole System

Companion notices patterns, understands what changed, and suggests one helpful next step.

- Notice: approved signals like sleep, movement, screen time, work switching, and rest breaks
- Understand: compare today with the user's normal routine
- Support: provide a recommendation, chat explanation, or breathing reset

# Why Different

Companion is not another mood tracker. It is a proactive wellness companion.

- Passive by design
- Cross-device view of daily life and work
- Action-oriented, not just charts

# Product Experience

A calm home screen, a clear forecast, and one useful action.

- Wellness Orbit
- Energy, focus, and stress scores
- Burnout forecast
- Companion chat
- Guided breathing reset

# Privacy & Trust

Wellness support should not feel like surveillance.

- No messages
- No screenshots
- No typed words
- User-approved signals
- Sensitive app skip list
- Delete today's logs or purge everything

# Demo

From everyday behavior to a useful wellness action.

- Start with consent and setup

# Business Model

Start with individuals, then expand to schools, companies, and wellness programs.

- Free: basic monitoring and privacy controls
- Premium: forecasts, deeper insights, companion explanations, and interventions
- Organizations: aggregate wellness trends with privacy protection

# Vision

Wellness technology should support people before problems become visible.

- For users: less logging, more useful support
- For organizations: earlier wellness signals without personal content
- For judges: a complete platform with a clear demo and growth path
